My story

I’m a Cincinnati-based UX Designer/Front End Developer who loves to make engaging and strategic ideas come to life for the user.

"Your work is going to fill a large part of your life, and the only way to be truly satisfied is to do what you believe is great work. And the only way to do great work is to love what you do. If you haven't found it yet, keep looking. Don't settle. As with all matters of the heart, you'll know when you find it."
—Steve Jobs

Since most designers are not developers, and vice versa, I take pride in the fact that I am well versed in one and continually learning and growing in the other. I enjoy the challenge of making beautiful interfaces become functional solutions.

In my free time you can find me trying out new craft beers, or hanging out with my boston terrier, Floyd.
